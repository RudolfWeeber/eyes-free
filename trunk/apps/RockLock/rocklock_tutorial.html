RockLock Tutorial

RockLock is a music player that can double as a lock screen. All of the music player controls are gesture-driven using linear gestures along the 8 compass directions. This tutorial defines the various gestures for each screen in RockLock.

RockLock Music Player Screen

On the main music player screen you can swipe left and right to navigate between tracks. You can swipe up and down to navigate between Artists, Albums, Genres, Playlists, etc. depending on your current navigation mode. By default, completing these gestures will one finger will automatically update the playing track. If you instead use two fingers, you can browse tracks without changing the current track, and tap when you are ready to play your new track. You can switch to single-finger browsing via the RockLock settings screen.

RockLock supports six distinct navigation modes:

  1. Artists: Navigate between artists and albums within artists.
  2. Albums: Navigate between albums.
  3. Genres: Navigate between music genres.
  4. Playlists: Navigate between playlists.
  5. Songs: Navigate between all tracks grouped alphabetically.
  6. Directories: Descend into and up from directories within sdcard/Music

You can switch between the various modes by swiping up-right to go to the next mode and down-left to go to the previous mode. Below is a list of all of the player actions and their associated gestures:

  • Toggle Pause/Play: TAP
  • Next Track: RIGHT (One Finger to Play. Two Fingers to Browse)
  • Previous Track: LEFT (One Finger to Play. Two Fingers to Browse)
  • Next Group: DOWN (One Finger to Play. Two Fingers to Browse)
  • Previous Group: UP (One Finger to Play. Two Fingers to Browse)
  • Next Navigation Mode: UP-RIGHT
  • Previous Navigation Mode: DOWN-LEFT
  • Add Current Song to Playlist: UP-LEFT
  • Add Bookmark for Current Song at Current Time: DOWN-RIGHT
  • Fast Forward/Rewind: CIRCLE (Clockwise / Counter-clockwise)

RockLock Menu Items

Additional RockLock features are all accessible via the menu button.

Menu Items:

  1. Toggle Shuffle: Shuffles within current navigation group.
  2. Search: Search music content.
  3. Playlists: Navigate and manage playlists.
  4. Bookmarks: Navigate and manage bookmarks.
  5. Settings: Toggle pre-lock setting. Toggle verbose mode (whether or not screen content is spoken aloud). Toggle two-finger vs. single-finger browsing.

Search Screen

The search screen uses the traditional eyes-free keyboard to enter search queries, so all single finger gestures are dedicated to typing. You can browse your search results by swiping up and down with two fingers. Swiping left and right with two fingers will navigate through different types of results (Song titles vs. Artists vs. Albums, etc.). Tap anywhere on the screen to play the current search result.

Playlist Manager Screen

  • Play Current Playlist Track: TAP
  • Next Playlist: DOWN
  • Previous Playlist: UP
  • New Playlist: RIGHT
  • Delete Current Playlist: LEFT
  • Browse Current Playlist Tracks: CIRCLE

Bookmarks Manager Screen

  • Play Current Bookmark: TAP
  • Next Bookmark: DOWN
  • Previous Bookmark: UP
  • Delete Bookmark: RIGHT or LEFT
