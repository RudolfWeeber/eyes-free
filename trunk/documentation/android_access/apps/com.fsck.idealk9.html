IDEAL Accessible K9 E-Mail

Description

IDEAL K9 E-Mail is an Android-based e-mail client that is accessible using Google's Talkback and other Android screenreaders.

This is based on the K9 E-Mail application, an open source email client based on the Email application shipped with the initial release of Android.

Free and open source as part of Raising the Floor.
