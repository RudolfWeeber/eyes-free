Rock Lock

Rock Lock is a simple, eyes-free music player for Android.
You can set Rock Lock as a pre-lock screen for your device to enable quick access to your music collection.
If pre-lock is enabled and you have a lock pattern, the Rock Lock will appear before you unlock your device.


Usage:

Touch the center of the screen and stroke for the various controls.

  • Tap = Play/Pause
  • Left = Previous track
  • Right = Next track
  • Up = Rewind
  • Down = Fast forward
  • Up-Left = Previous artist
  • Up-Right = Next artist
  • Down-Left = Previous album
  • Down-Right = Next album

Settings:

You can use Rock Lock as a pre-lock screen.

If you turn on the pre-lock option:
If you have a lock pattern set - you will get Rock Lock when you turn the screen on. After dismissing Rock Lock, you will find yourself on the lock pattern screen.
If you do not have a lock pattern set - you will get Rock Lock when you turn the screen on. After dismissing Rock Lock, you will find that your device is unlocked.

If you leave the pre-lock option off:
Rock Lock will have no effect on your lockscreen.
