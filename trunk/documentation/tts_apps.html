Bubble

by Ben Zibble

Website: http://www.ktk.bz

Price: Free

A simple bubble level. Hold any of the phone's four sides against an object to test it for plumb/level, or lay it down on a flat surface for a 360° level.

Play PowerVocab and build the vocabulary of an advanced reader! You will master challenging words commonly seen in newspapers, general interest magazines and books. Now with multiple quiz types and pronunciation. Coming soon: Word packs for advanced users and test-takers.

Preschooler

by Ben Zibble

Website: http://www.ktk.bz

Price: Free

A talking educational flashcard game for teaching children the alphabet. Two letters are shown, the name of one is spoken. Touch it and see a photo of an animal representing that letter.
- NEW: button lock
- 26 photos, one for each letter
- male or female voice
- auto screen rotation

SMSpeaker

by IDEAL Group, Inc. Android Development Team

Website: http://apps4android.mobi

Price: $0.99

Ever wish your Android device could read your SMS messages out to you? Then SMSpeaker is for you!
Note: SMSpeaker uses Google's Text-To-Speech (TTS) library. If you do not have it installed already, SMSpeaker will first ask you to install the TTS. Google's TTS is a free download.

Speaking Pad

TTS Translator

by Dennis Miller

Price: Free

TTS Translator allows you to input words or phrases in one language and see the translated result in a textual representation as well as being spoken back to you by the eSpeak synthesized voice engine. Includes 14 different language combinations, history management and SMS and email integration.

The vOICe

by Peter Meijer

Website: http://www.seeingwithsound.com/android.htm

Price: Free

The vOICe for Android translates live camera views into sound, targeting augmented reality for the totally blind through sensory substitution and synthetic vision. Includes a talking color identifier and talking compass. Needs JIT compiler for better speed performance. More info at http://www.seeingwithsound.com/android.htm

Watts

An app that keeps a track of your battery charge level and plots it for you over time.
